My Service

Montgomery County Food Bank

I volunteered at the Montgomery County Food Bank from February 2022 to May 2024, completing over 60 hours of service. During that time, I tried out a variety of roles, including general sorting, the Produce Rescue Center, the Meat Lovers shift, and Mobile Markets. After reaching 50 hours, I earned the Red Shirt role, which recognized my commitment and let me take on more responsibility.
